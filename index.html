Above All Music Presents

Risze is an up and coming artist, born and raised in Chicago's very own Englewood neighborhood. He started off writing poetry at the mere age of 8 years old, and from there developed a passion for Hip Hop and its evolving culture. Once in college, he decided to transition his artistic talents to rap. Today, Risze can be considered one of the essential missing pieces to the Hip Hop community.
